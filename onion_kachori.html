ONION KACHORI



In frame onnion kachori is famous across india and is made with myriad stuffing including peas, moong dal and potatoes.
but this recipe of kachori is made with an easy and simple stuffing of spiced onions.


INGREDIENTS :


  • 3 tsp oil

  • ½ tsp cumin / jeera, ½ tsp coriander seeds, crushed, ½ tsp fennel / saunf

  • pinch hing / asafoetida, 1 chilli, finely chopped

  • ½ tsp ginger paste

  • 2 onion, roughly chopped

  • Maida

  • Salt as per taste


    • STEPS ONE CAN FOLLOW(if needed) :


  • First take all purpose flour, add oil and salt and make a dough

  • Then start with making stuffing, add oil to the pan and heat, then add all the onions chopped and ginger

  • Then add all the masalas mentioned above, then cook it well until light brown

  • Then cool it down, start gimaking th kachori's,and put the oil to heat for frying

  • After giving the shape , start frying, then after frying serve it with chutneys or ketchup
