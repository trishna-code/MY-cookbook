MAWA MODAK



In frame is Mawa Modak,it is a sweet dish made from sweetened Mawa/khoya (dried milk or milk solids).
I had added little bit kesar to it, one can add whatever nuts or flavouring they would want in there peda/modak.


INGREDIENTS :


  • 1 cup milk

  • sugar as per taste

  • 1 pinch of kesar

  • milk powder

  • modak shaper

  • 2-3 chooped almonds


    • STEPS ONE CAN FOLLOW(if needed) :


  • First take a bowl and add kesar to hot milk and keep it aside for 20 minutes

  • Then take a pan and heat it with oil / ghee and then add kesar milk and cook a little

  • After that add milk powder and continuesly stir and then add sugar as per taste and chopped almonds and
    get it to a stage that it becomes more sticky and is not liquidy

  • Then let it cool down properly, after thatbtake the modak shaper and add the stuff and before doing
    that add little ghee or oil to the modak shaper

  • Then keep them in the fridge to set them properly
