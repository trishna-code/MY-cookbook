BAKED MUSHROOM



In frame is Stuffed mushrooms is a dish prepared using edible mushrooms as its primary ingredient.
Many fillings can be used, and the dish is typically baked or broiled.
It can be served hot or at room temperature,


INGREDIENTS :


  • Medium sized button mushrooms,finely chopped onion tomato and cilantro

  • Any grated cheese, salt, rock salt,black pepper

  • Garam masala,chat masala,turmeric powder and dhaniya powder

  • 1 spoon butter and oil

  • Ginger garlic finely chopped


    • STEPS ONE CAN FOLLOW(if needed) :


  • first chop the mushroom stem finely

  • Then add 1 spoon oil to the pan and the add ginger garlic paste,onion and tomato and roast them well

  • Then add finely chopped garlic to 1 spoon butter and toss the mushrooms and let it cool

  • After that fill the mushroom with the studding that was made before and then put cheese on top and let it melt

  • Then garnish with little bit chopped cilantro and serve with any dip
