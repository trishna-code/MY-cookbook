INDIAN CURRIES

Curry is a variety of dishes originating in the Indian subcontinent. It uses a combination of spices or herbs, usually including ground turmeric, cumin, coriander, ginger, and fresh or dried chilies and any more combination of indian spices. Curry is generally prepared as a sauce.
