VEGGIES ROLL



In frame is vegetable roll that has stuffing of vegetable and have been baked.ine can add cheese to it too or else can only make with veggies.


INGREDIENTS :


  • 1 chopped capsicum,onion and 1/2 cabbage chopped

  • Maida ,refined oil

  • 1 tablespoon salt,black pepper and black salt

  • Chipotle sauce

  • Curd and luke warm water

  • Milk


    • STEPS ONE CAN FOLLOW(if needed) :


  • First take a pan and heat 2 spoon oil and then add the veggies and cook well, add 1 tablespoon salt,
    black pepper and black salt as well

  • For the dough add 1 spoon curd, luke warm water, Maida,oil, baking soda and baking powder, keep it aside for an hour

  • After that give a shape to the rolls with the stuffing and on top brush up with milk

  • Then bake the roll at 180 C for 30 minutes
