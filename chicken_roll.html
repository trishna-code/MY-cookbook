BAKED CHICKEN ROLL



In frame is a type of chicken roll baked in oven.These chicken rolls have a stuffing of chicken with some sauce and some vegetables.


INGREDIENTS :


  • Maida,oil

  • baking soda and baking powder

  • 1 spoon curd, luke warm water

  • 500g minced chicken

  • Chipotle sauce

  • Salt,black pepper

  • egg white or milk

  • 1 chopped capsicum and onion


    • STEPS ONE CAN FOLLOW(if needed) :


  • First take a pan and heat 2 spoon oil, then add veggies, black pepper and salt,let it cook

  • Then add minced chicken and cook well and let it down to cool

  • After that add chipotle sauce to it and then make the dough

  • For the dough add 1 spoon curd, luke warm water, Maida,oil, baking soda and baking powder, keep it aside for an hour

  • Then give a wash of milk or egg on top of the roll, and then bake at 180 C for 20 minutes
