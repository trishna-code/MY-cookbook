ZINGY PARCEL



In frame is Zingy Parcel ,a distinguished snack for Kids as well as foodies.
It has a triangular pocket like shape, which is filled with deliciously seasoned stuffing.


INGREDIENTS :


  • 500g Paneer cubes

  • Kasturi Methi,salt,chilli powder,turmeric powder,dhaniya powder and garam masala

  • Refined oil

  • Maida

  • 1 spoon curd,baking soda,baking powder and luke warm water

  • Harissa / chipotle sauce

  • Oregano, salt


    • STEPS ONE CAN FOLLOW(if needed) :


  • First take a pan and heat 1 spoon oil, then toss the paneer cubes with the masalas

  • Then take maida and add 1 spoon curd,baking soda,baking powder and luke warm water and make a dough
    and keep it covered for 1 hour

  • After all this is done Roll the dough into triangles add harissa sauce and keep some paneer cubes and give it a shape

  • Then bake the zingy parcel at 200 C for 20-25 minutes
