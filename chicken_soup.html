CHICKEN SOUP



In frame is Chicken soup , a soup made from chicken, simmered in water, usually with various other ingredients.
The classic chicken soup consists of a clear chicken broth, often with pieces of chicken or vegetables, noodles, dumplings,


INGREDIENTS :


  • 500g boneless chicken

  • chooped carrot,capsicum,cabbage

  • 1 spoon vinegar,chiili sauce,tomato sauce and soy sauce

  • cilantro/dhaniya leaves

  • salt as per taste

  • 1/2 spoon black pepper,rock salt
