BARBEQUE CHICKEN



In frame is Barbecue chicken consists of chicken parts or entire chickens that are barbecued, grilled or smoked.
There are many global and regional preparation techniques and cooking styles.


INGREDIENTS :


  • Pre-cut chicken 1 kg

  • Veeba Barbeque sauce, 1 spoon butter and 1 spoon oil

  • Ginger and garlic paste, red chilli powder, salt

  • Roasted sesame seeds

  • Chopped spring onion


    • STEPS ONE CAN FOLLOW(if needed) :


  • First marinate chicken with salt and ginger garlic paste

  • Take one spoon oil and butter on a heated pan and then put the chicken

  • Roast the chicken well and then add barbeque sauce on the chicekn and turn off the gas

  • Then at the end garnish the chicken with some sesame seeds and chopped spring onion
